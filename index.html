I am a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Visual Computing at Saarland University. My current research focuses on ill-posed problems at the intersection of computer vision, graphics, and machine learning, supervised by Jan Eric Lennsen at Max Plack Institute for Informatics, Germany.

Before this, I worked as Research Assistant at Spectrum Lab, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), India under the supervision of   Chandra Sekhar and Harish Kumar JR focused on Biomedical Image Processing.
